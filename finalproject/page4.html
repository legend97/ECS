Item Type Uses
Gold Type of currency Gold can be used to buy cards, as well as upgrade them too.
Gems Type of Currency Gems can be used to buy chests, and also buy gold.
Cards Item Cards can be bought from the shop buy using gems or gold. Cards can also most commonly be acquired from chests.
Chests Item Chests can be bought from the shop using gems (and on rare occasions, gold). Chests may contain a combinations of gems, gold, or cards.

The shop is a page in the game where you can replenish your resources when you're running low. Players usually want to buy rare cards in the shop, and also rare chests in the shop.
