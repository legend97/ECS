Donations

Every time a person requests for some cards (40 common cards or 4 rare cards) their clanmates can donate. Every sunday, however, people are allowed to request for epic cards. A player gets gold and experience for cards that they donate.

Your Clan

Clans are a feature where players can congregate and try to push themselves to their best potential while receiving and giving help from friends and competing with other clans, to prove their skill in Clash Royale. In a clan, you are able to share replays from both your own battle log and TV Royale.

Another important feature in clans is the donations and card request feature. In this feauture, clanmates can requests for cards that they want. Their clanmates will donate cards, and then they will get gold and experience as a reward.

Profile

The profile page in clash Royale is a public page that everybody can see. The Profile page contatins the following information: tophy count, total wins, their clan, total doations, favorite card and current battle deck.

Your profile and clan will be the social part of clash royale. This way, you will be able to see your friends profile, as well as connect to them and join or compete with each others clans.
