Building

There are a total of 11 buildings in clash royale, which can be catagorized into 2 categorize. Passive buidling, and Active buildings. Passive buidlings are buildings that don't input any damage themselves. These can include the goblin hut, the furnace, the barbarian hut, the tombstone, and the elixer collector. The other type of building is active. These building directly damage enemy troops and directly inputs damage. This can include the inferno tower, the xbow, the mortar, the tesla, the cannon, and the bomb tower.

Troops

There are a total of 51 troops. Troops are phystical beings that have different properties and stats. Main statistics that vary between troops is damage, health, speed, and targets. Troops could be split into 3 main categories: ground targeting, air tageting, and tower targeting. Gournd targting troops can only target other ground troops. These consists of barbarians, elite barbarians, the knight, the goblins, the bomber, the mini pekka, the valkerie, the skeletons, the guards, the dark prince, the skeleton army, the bowler, the prince, the giant skeleton, the pekka, the sparky, the maga knight, the bandit, the night witch, and the lumberjack. Air targeting troops could attack ground and air. These include the ice spirit, the bats, the fire spirits, the spear goblins the archers, the minions, the goblin gang, the minion horder, the mega minion, the dart goblin, the flying machine, the wizard, the mucketeer, the 3 musketeers, the baby dragon, the witch, the executioner, the princess, the ice wizard, the electrowizard, and the inferno dragon.

Spells

In Clash Royale, there are a total of 15 spells. Spells are a type of card in clash royale in which the player conjures a feature in a specific area. For example, if a player were to put a fireball in a specific area, then enemy troops in that area will be damages(depending on how much damage the fireball does.) There are 2 types of spells, active sppells and passive spells, active spells directly damage the enemy troops and towers. Active spells consist of the the fireball, the lightning, the rocket, the poison, the zap, the arrows and the log. Passive spells don't damage eney troops or towers. These consists of the tornado, the freeze, the goblin barrel, the mirror, the clone, the rage , the graveyard and the heal.

There are 3 main types of cards that you can use to create the best possible deck that fits your playstyle:

  • Buildings, which stay in place
  • Troops, which can move around
  • Spells, which can be placed in a certatin area to do damage
