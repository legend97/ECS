Supercell

Supercell is a mobile game developer based in Helsinki, Finland, with offices in San Francisco, Tokyo, Seoul and Shanghai. Its games are available for tablets and smartphones using both Apple’s iOS and Google’s Android operating systems. Since its founding in 2010, Supercell has brought four games to the market - Hay Day, Clash of Clans, Boom Beach and Clash Royale.

Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a fast-paced Player v. Player game. It was developed by Supercell. It was released globally in March 2, 2016. In Clash Royale, players are ranked by their experience level and trophy count. Players level up by gaining experience points through donating and/or upgrading cards and completing achievements. The highest possible level is level thirteen. As players progress and gain more trophies, they also progress through the 11 arenas. Trophies are won or lost through multiplayer battles - a player wins a battle by destroying more towers than the opponent (each destroyed tower being represented as a 'crown'), or by destroying the opponent's King's Tower, resulting in an automatic "three-crown" victory. As they climb to higher arena's they're able to acquire more cards in chests.

How to Play

To battle, the player will have four of the 8 cards at the bottom of screen. To place down your cards, you tap on the card to select it, and tap anywhere on the screen to place down your card. There are certain places where you can and can't place certain cards. There are also many different pages in which you can socialize, compete, and buy resources.(click the video to go to youtube to make it bigger.)
